    Exploding pagers

    The news of Hezbollah agents being targeted with exploding pagers and UHF radios was a shock to us all. Whilst this type of attack was always a possibility in theory, seeing it occur in practice was perhaps the most visually horrific instance of a supply chain attack in recent history. This attack, along with the .mobi WHOIS debacle and the faulty Crowdstrike update, got me thinking quite a lot about supply chain attack surface.
